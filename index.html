About Me

Isaac Dowdle is an Arizona native with a passion for performance and entertainment. His favorite credits include Prince Topher in Cinderella, Wild Bill Hickok in Calamity Jane, Chauvelin in Scarlet Pimpernel, and George Novak in She Loves Me. From tours to half-time shows Isaac has performed in China, Argentina, Mexcio, California, Nevada, Louisiana, Massachusetts, Arizona, Texas, and Utah. When away from the stage, he enjoys loud get-togethers with friends and family, board games, tennis, and collecting Legos.
